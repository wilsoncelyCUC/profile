# Current Value Proposition

I help teams own product outcomes end-to-end, from problem framing to shipped value.

The core offer is product engineering with outcome ownership: one accountable operator model that connects product decisions, engineering execution, and business impact.

## Ideal Customers (ICP)

The current website positions you for three segments:

- SME operators
- High-paced startup teams
- Enterprise innovation and product departments

### Segment Fit (as presented on the site)

- SME Operators: lean teams needing one person to connect roadmap choices, implementation, and business priorities.
- High-Paced Startups: scaling teams that need high velocity without losing quality or focus.
- Enterprise Innovation/Product Teams: departments that must turn pilots into production outcomes with governance and executive clarity.

## Core Problem You Solve

The current messaging frames a single strategic problem: teams are not blocked by ideas, they are blocked by ownership gaps.

Recurring pain points:

- Too many ideas and no decision system
- Work ships but outcomes stay unclear
- Cross-functional drag between product, data, and engineering

## Your Differentiator

The active services section defines your position clearly:

- You own user and business outcomes, not only implementation output.
- You connect product choices, engineering tradeoffs, and delivery execution.
- You use metrics to learn, iterate, and prioritize what matters next.

And it explicitly rejects:

- Ticket-only implementation
- Pure strategy without shipping
- Staff augmentation without accountability for impact

## Services (Active Offers)

- Outcome: A ranked execution plan with explicit tradeoffs, ownership, and the next 90 days de-risked.

  - Feasibility screen across architecture, data, and dependencies

- Format: Focused sprint (2-3 weeks)
- CTA: Request scope
